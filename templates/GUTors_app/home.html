Welcome

To our tutoring page for Glasgow University Students! GUTors is a tutor matching website for University of Glasgow (UofG) students, helping them find suitable tutors on campus. Typically, Tutors are fourth or fifth-year students and the Students are those who are looking for guidance on study. The students will have the ability to book sessions with tutors and review these sessions. Tutors can be search for based on their name, average session rating, subjects they teach and/or their availability.
