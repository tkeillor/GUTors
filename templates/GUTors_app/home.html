About GUTors

GUTors is a tutor-matching website for University of Glasgow (UofG) students, helping them find suitable tutors on campus. Tutors are typically fourth or fifth-year students, while students seeking tutoring can book and review their sessions.

Why Use GUTors?

Find the Right Tutor

Search by name, subjects and ratings.

Book Sessions

Schedule 1-on-1 or group tutoring sessions at your convenience.

Leave Reviews

Rate your tutoring sessions to help other students.

How It Works

  • 1. Sign up and create your profile.
  • 2. Search for tutors by name, subject, or rating.
  • 3. Book a tutoring session at a time that suits you.
  • 4. After the session, leave a review to help others.
